MINISTRY IMPLEMENTATION.
Daniella | 13 years old

Mission of Mercy (CMR) has served the community since 2003, and has continued to provide care to the under-privileged children on a “ Foster care Model”, due to limited funds for buildings infrastructures for the ministry. The ministry has served a total of 50 children, many of whom have graduated from colleges and universities, and some others are is still in high school and primary school. . see testimonies. The ministry had purchased a land, and we are trusting The Lord for finances in order to start building a home for the children. Mission of Mercy(CMR) local team in Cameroon continues to reach out to the poor and orphans in a monthly basis, and provide foods and other supplies to them regularly.

LOCATION

is a country in the Gulf of Guinea, on the western facade of Africa. It has 590 km27 of very jagged coastline along the Atlantic Ocean. Very extensive in latitude (1,200 km from north to south), the country has schematically the shape of a triangle, the base of which runs along the 2nd degree of north latitude, while the summit, bordering Lake Chad, reaches the 13th parallel. Cameroon is surrounded by the following countries and bodies of water: Bidim dam in Cameroon Nigeria and the Atlantic Ocean to the west. Equatorial Guinea, Gabon and the Republic of Congo to the south. The Central African Republic and Chad to the east. Lake Chad to the north. By its area of ​​475,442 km2 - 465,400 km2 of land surface27 - and its population of approximately 19,598,889 inhabitants in 2010, Cameroon is a medium-sized country in Africa. The country lies between the southern edge of the Sahara and the northern limit of the equatorial forest of the Congo Basin to the south. The west of the country is dominated by the Hauts-Plateaux, and includes the highest massif in all of West Africa: Mount Cameroon, which rises to 407,028 meters; it is the ninth summit of the African continent. The eastern part of the country is covered for the most part by a still well preserved equatorial forest. Along its 590 km of coastline, there are some seaside resorts: Kribi, and Limbé near Mount Cameroon.
